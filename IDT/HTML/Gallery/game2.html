Overview of Grand Theft Auto

The Grand Theft Auto series is one of the most influential and controversial franchises in gaming history. Known for its open‑world freedom, satirical storytelling, and cultural impact, GTA has shaped modern gaming for over two decades. The series reached unprecedented popularity with GTA V, which became one of the best‑selling games of all time and maintained relevance for over a decade.

The Peak: GTA V

Released in 2013, GTA V became a global phenomenon. Its massive open world, three‑protagonist system, and the explosive success of GTA Online kept the game alive for years. It broke sales records, dominated Twitch and YouTube, and became a cultural icon.

The Long‑Awaited Arrival: GTA VI

After years of speculation, Rockstar officially revealed GTA VI, instantly becoming one of the most anticipated games in history. With a return to Vice City, next‑gen visuals, and a new duo of protagonists, the hype surrounding GTA VI marks a new era for the franchise.
