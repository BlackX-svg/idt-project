World of Airports

Released: October 22 2019

First Airport: Innsbruck, Austria (INN)

Latest Airport: Zurich, Switzerland (ZRH)

Developer: FlyBoys Games

Genre: Airport Management, Simulation, Strategy

Overview of World of Airports

World of Airports is a real‑time airport management game where players operate and expand airports around the world. You control everything from handling arrivals and departures to managing ground crews, upgrading terminals, and unlocking new aircraft. Each airport—like HKG, BKK, LHR, offers unique layouts and challenges as you grow your fleet and efficiency. The game blends strategy, progression, and realistic aviation detail, letting players build a global network while watching planes taxi, park, and take off in real time.

The Best Airports

World of Airports has introduced many hubs over time, but these three airports stand above the rest as the definitive best of the game’s design, realism, and gameplay flow. These airports offer unmatched traffic density, aircraft diversity, and visual detail — especially with Hong Kong’s newly completed terminal and runway system.

HKG – Hong Kong International Airport

The crown jewel of World of Airports. HKG is displayed in its completed three‑runway system, including the new terminal — making it the most visually impressive and operationally intense airport in the game. Its nonstop mix of cargo and passenger traffic creates the perfect high‑density gameplay loop.

  • Massive widebody operations (CX, UPS, FedEx, Air China, Emirates)
  • High‑volume cargo traffic, especially at night
  • Three‑runway realism with heavy parallel operations
  • One of the best airports for grinding income

BKK – Bangkok Suvarnabhumi

BKK is a fan‑favorite for its nonstop regional and long‑haul mix. The airport layout is efficient, visually clean, and perfect for players who enjoy high‑tempo operations without overwhelming chaos.

  • Strong mix of A320, A330, B777, and B787 traffic
  • Excellent mid‑game progression airport
  • High route diversity across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East
  • Fast turnaround times and smooth taxi flow

LHR – London Heathrow

LHR is the ultimate widebody playground. As one of the busiest airports in the world, it delivers nonstop heavy aircraft operations, iconic liveries, and some of the most rewarding long‑haul routes in the game.

  • Best widebody density (A350, B777, B787, A380)
  • High‑value long‑haul routes to every continent
  • Iconic airlines: British Airways, Virgin Atlantic, Emirates, Qatar
  • Perfect for late‑game fleet expansion

Why These Airports Are Considered The Best

  • Strongest mix of aircraft types and route lengths
  • High replay value due to nonstop traffic
  • Balanced income progression
  • Iconic real‑world hubs with global connections
  • Most visually detailed airports in the game

Most Anticipated Future Airports

The community is eagerly awaiting several major global hubs that would redefine the game’s scale and traffic density. These airports are expected to bring new gameplay challenges, massive widebody operations, and iconic real‑world aviation environments.

  • SIN – Singapore Changi – One of the world’s best airports, perfect for Asia‑Pacific traffic.
  • ICN – Seoul Incheon – A major Korean Air and Asiana hub with heavy long‑haul operations.
  • LAX – Los Angeles – The busiest West Coast airport with insane airline variety.
  • CDG – Paris Charles de Gaulle – A massive Air France hub with complex runway operations.
  • AMS – Amsterdam Schiphol – KLM’s home base with iconic parallel runway systems.
  • FRA – Frankfurt – Lufthansa’s global hub and one of Europe’s busiest airports.

Legacy & Community Impact

World of Airports has built a dedicated community of aviation fans, airport managers, and aircraft collectors. Its blend of realism, strategy, and satisfying progression has made it one of the most beloved mobile aviation games ever created — and the upcoming airports promise to push the game even further.
